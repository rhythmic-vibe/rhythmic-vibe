About Us

OUR MISSION

Welcome to Rhythmic Vibe Kathak Dance Academy â€“ Where Passion Meets Tradition. It is a Classical Dance Academy which offers Studio and Online Training for kathak Enthusiasts. The school conducts exams and offers certification from the recognized University of India. In an environment of movement, music, culture and fun, students are introduced to the basics of Kathak dance, including rhythm, mathematics, body vocabulary, the spoken language (called Bols) and the stories from historic context. Our School has performed in various festivals in Canada such as Panorama, Carrassauga, and many more cultural events. In our Academy, we are dedicated to preserving and sharing the beauty of Indian Classical Dance, specifically Kathak, with students of all ages and skill levels. Founded by passionate instructor, our school provides a nurturing environment for students to explore their creativity, develop discipline, and connect with the rich cultural heritage of Kathak. Whether you are a child or an Adult, we are here to help you grow and express yourself through the art of dance. Join us today and be part of a vibrant community of dancers who inspire, learn, and grow together!

DIVE INTO OUR STORY

Our Director- Founder and Teacher Mrs.Amrita Mukherjee is a Trained Classical Kathak Dancer with 20+ years of experience. She is a Sangeet Ratna in Kathak and Sangeet Bhaskar in Bharatnatyam. She founded "Rhythmic Vibe" in 2019, based in Burlington (Ontario) Canada. She and her Students have performed at various events across Canada and India. She began her training in a small town in India at the age of 7 with Bharatnatyam under Ashok Nandi and Meena Nandi - disciple of "Shri.Uday Shankar" and later pursued Kathak dancing under Shri. Vipul Chandra Das. Presently she is a disciple of Guru Smt. Moli Siddharth, who is the prime disciple of "Pandit.Birju Maharaj", also the Founder-Director of "Kalaangan" Academy based in Mumbai, India. She believes "Learning is a lifelong process of keeping your mind open to new ideas and experiences" and She sets an exemplary standard as a student and teacher, marked by dedication, discipline, and passion.
